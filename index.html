Osun Hackathon

Osun Hackathon is an exciting competition that challenges participants to use their coding, design, and problem-solving skills to address pressing issues facing our community and beyond. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just getting started, we welcome participants of all skill levels to join us on this exciting journey.

At Osun Hackathon, we're passionate about fostering innovation and technology-driven solutions that can transform our community, our state, and the world. Our mission is to bring together the brightest minds, creative thinkers, and tech enthusiasts to collaborate and create impactful solutions for real-world challenges.



Why Participate?

Opportunity to make a difference: Your innovative ideas could have a positive impact on society. Network with experts: Connect with like-minded individuals, mentors, and potential collaborators. Prizes and Recognition: Win amazing prizes and get recognized for your outstanding work. Learning Experience: Enhance your skills, learn from industry experts, and gain valuable experience. Don't miss this incredible opportunity to be part of a dynamic and innovative community. Register now and embark on a journey of creativity, collaboration, and positive impact.

Eligibility:

  • The competition is open to individuals and teams.
  • Participants must be at least 18 years old.
  • All participants must register in advance.

Project Requirements:

  • Projects must address one of the specified themes.
  • All code and designs must be created during the competition.
  • Projects must be submitted in English.

Team Formation:

  • Teams can consist of 1 to 3 members.
  • All code and designs must be created during the competition.
